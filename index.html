Campus Life

School Auditorium

School Auditorium
School has two auditoriums, a seminar hall, two multipurpose halls and an amphitheater. Two well equipped air conditioned 'Auditoriums' and a 'Seminar Hall' provide ample opportunities for students to showcase their talents through various activities, presentations and talks.

School Library

School Library
Amity Noida has four libraries stocked with ample reference material in the form of books, periodicals, science journals and CDs with multimedia content for both junior and senior students. Each library has computer consoles with broadband connectivity to allow students instant, (supervised) access to the knowledge based on the internet.
